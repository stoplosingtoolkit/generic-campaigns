When he returned, he told God, "Yes, it is bad on earth; 95 percent are misbehaving and only 5 percent are not."

Work Better With The Web

In a FastCompany video series, Gina Trapani, founder of Lifehacker, shares these tips on living in a tech world:
  • Master your social-media life if you have Facebook, Twitter and more. Manage multiple accounts by funneling alerts, interacting with a single interface, and designating each as business or pleasure.
  • Manage your email inbox by treating it like a post office box. Empty it every time you check it. Have a filing system for emails on various subjects.
  • Remember that Google is now the pre-interview. What you do online tells present and future employers as much about you as your resume does.
